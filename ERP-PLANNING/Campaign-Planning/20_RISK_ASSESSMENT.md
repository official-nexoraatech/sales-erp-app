## Overall Risk Posture

The highest-severity items (R3 webhook spoofing, R8 tenant isolation, R9 compliance) are all things this
codebase's existing conventions already handle well elsewhere (permission checks, tenant scoping,
audit logging) — the risk is inconsistency in applying those conventions to new surface area, not a novel
unsolved problem. R1, R7, and R10 are risks specifically because they are _proven recurring patterns in this
exact codebase's history_ (per the memory-system entries cited) — treat them as the highest-attention items
during code review at the end of each phase.
